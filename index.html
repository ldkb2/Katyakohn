About the Artist

Working across oils, watercolor, charcoal, and silk, Katya Kohn's work explores the interplay of form, color, and light — from bold gestural abstractions to contemplative studies of shape and space. Born in Ecuador in 1942, she studied at Bard College and Central Saint Martins in London. She lives in Geneva, Switzerland.
